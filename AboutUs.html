About DESS

At DESS, we specialize in the design, installation, and service of integrated security management, closed-circuit television, photo ID badging, and network communication systems. We are a full-service provider committed to safety, innovation, and excellence. DESS was established in 2003 and had been serving the tristate area for over 20 years.
